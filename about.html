About

After graduating from the University of North Texas with a BA in History with honors, I enrolled in Syracuse University to learn programing. I obtained two certificates through the College of Professional Studies at Syracuse University, Full Stack Development and Esports Gaming.

I enjoy coding Websites and Web Applications. The building process is interesting to me due to just how much can be included on a website. I like seeing how a website comes together.

Designing Banners and Logos for Twitch Streaming is something that I’ve done for my own channel on Twitch. Throughout the process, I was able to gain the skills needed to accomplish this goal through the guidance of a YouTube tutorial such as being able to use a photo editing software and a drawing tablet.

I’m interested in creating Assets and Maps for Video Games. I made an unofficial map for a video game called City Skylines that I shared on the City Skylines Workshop. I enjoyed learning how the editor works and sculpting the map. This interest is one that is shared with my love for level design.

Writing is something that I’ve been interested in for a while. There are a lot of different avenues even in the creative writing field such as quest and dialogues in video games.
